Classic Jollof Rice

Home

Jollof Rice is a beloved West African dish where long-grain rice is simmered gently in a rich, flavorful tomato and pepper sauce until every grain is perfectly tender.

Ingredients

  • Parboiled Rice
  • Tomatoes and Peppers
  • Tomato Paste
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Onions
  • Seasoning Cubes

Steps

  1. Blend: Puree the tomatoes, bell peppers, and onions until completely smooth.
  2. Fry: Heat oil in a deep pot and fry sliced onions, then stir in tomato paste.
  3. Cook Sauce: Pour in the blended mix and fry until the oil separates from the sauce.
  4. Season: Add your spices, seasoning cubes, salt, and bay leaves into the pot.
  5. Wash: Rinse your rice thoroughly under warm water until the water runs clear.
  6. Combine: Pour the washed rice and chicken broth into the seasoned sauce.
  7. Simmer: Cover tightly with foil and a lid, turning the heat down to low.
  8. Steam: Let it cook for 30 minutes until the rice absorbs all the liquid.
