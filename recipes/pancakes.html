Fluffy Pancakes

Home

Pancakes are a classic, comforting breakfast food. This simple recipe creates thick, light, and fluffy pancakes with golden edges ready for syrup.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Mix Dry: Whisk the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt together in a bowl.
  2. Mix Wet: Whisk the milk, egg, and melted butter together in another bowl.
  3. Combine: Pour the wet ingredients into the dry bowl and stir gently until combined.
  4. Rest: Let the batter sit for 5 minutes while you heat up your frying pan.
  5. Pour: Scoop a small amount of batter onto a lightly greased hot pan.
  6. Flip: Cook until bubbles pop on top, then flip and cook the other side until golden.
